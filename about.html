Our Story

Preserving football history through authentic retro kits since 2018

About Iconic Threads

Founded in 2018, Iconic Threads began with a passion for football heritage and a mission to bring iconic football kits from the past back to life for fans around the world.

What started as a small collection of vintage replicas has grown into a globally recognized brand, known for our attention to detail, quality craftsmanship, and dedication to authenticity.

Our carefully curated selection of retro football kits spans decades of football history, allowing fans to connect with the rich heritage of the beautiful game while wearing a piece of that history.

Our Values

Authenticity

We're committed to creating the most authentic replicas possible, capturing the essence of each era's iconic designs.

Quality

Every kit we produce uses premium materials and attention to detail, ensuring durability and comfort.

Football Heritage

We believe in preserving and celebrating the rich history of football through its most iconic kits.

Our Process

We meticulously research original kits through archives, photos, and working with collectors to ensure every detail is accurate.

Our expert pattern makers create precise templates that capture the unique cut and fit of each era's football kits.

We source high-quality fabrics that match the look and feel of original kits while providing modern comfort and durability.

Every kit goes through rigorous quality checks to ensure it meets our high standards before reaching our customers.
