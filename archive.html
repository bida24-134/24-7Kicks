KIT ARCHIVE

Explore our collection of iconic football kits through the decades

2002-2003

Auxerre 2002 Home Kit

Adidas

The classic white design worn during Auxerre's European campaign. Notable for its clean design and blue collar trim, this kit saw the emergence of Djibril Cissé as a top striker in France.

2006-2008

Portugal Home Kit

Nike

Worn during the Euro 2008 campaign by Cristiano Ronaldo. The burgundy design with gold trim became iconic for Portuguese football and represented the golden generation's latter years.

1988-1990

West Germany Home Kit

Adidas

The iconic design worn during their 1990 World Cup victory. The distinctive flag-inspired pattern across the chest has become one of football's most recognizable designs and influenced later kits.

World Cup Winners
1999-2000

West Ham United Home Kit

Fila

The classic claret and blue kit worn during the 1999-2000 season. This design saw a young Rio Ferdinand and Joe Cole emerging as stars while the club finished in a strong Premier League position.

Dr. Martens
2004-2005

Real Madrid Home Kit

Adidas

The classic white kit worn during the Galácticos era featuring Zidane, Ronaldo, Beckham, and Figo. The sleek design with navy and gold accents embodied the glamour of the star-studded Madrid squad.

Galácticos Era
1995-1996

Manchester United Away Kit

Umbro

The infamous grey away kit that was changed at half-time against Southampton when Ferguson claimed players couldn't see each other. Despite its controversial history, it became a cult classic among collectors.

Infamous Kit
2007-2008

AC Milan Home Kit

Adidas

The classic rossoneri stripes worn by Kaká, Pirlo, and Maldini during their Champions League campaign following their 2007 victory. Features the iconic red and black vertical stripes that define Milan's identity.

1996-1997

Inter Milan Away Kit

Umbro

The iconic blue and white diagonal split design worn by Ronaldo during his debut season at Inter. This distinctive kit is remembered for its bold asymmetrical design that broke traditional kit conventions.
