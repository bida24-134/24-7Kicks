Classic Kits Collection

Relive the golden era of football with these iconic designs from the 90s and early 2000s

1999-2000

Lazio Home Kit

Puma

The sky blue kit worn during Lazio's historic 1999-2000 Scudetto-winning season. This kit saw the likes of Nesta, Nedvěd, and Verón lead the team to their second Serie A title.

Serie A Champions
2007-2008

Manchester United Away Kit

Nike

The iconic black away kit worn during United's historic 2007-08 Champions League-winning season. This design was worn by Ronaldo, Rooney, and Scholes during their Champions League triumph in Moscow against Chelsea.

Champions League
2012

Netherlands Away Kit

Nike

The striking black away kit worn during Euro 2012 in Poland and Ukraine. This design featured the Dutch squad with Van Persie, Robben, and Sneijder as they aimed to follow up their 2010 World Cup final appearance.

Euro 2012
1995-1996

Borussia Dortmund Away Kit

Nike

The striking away kit from Dortmund's Champions League-winning season of 1996-97. Worn by legends like Matthias Sammer and Jürgen Kohler during their European triumph.

Champions League
1996-1997

Manchester United Away Kit

Umbro

The white away kit with black and red trim worn during the 1996-97 season when United won the Premier League title. This distinctive design featured the Sharp Viewcam sponsor and was worn during their successful campaign.

Premier League Winners
1997

Palmeiras Home Kit

Rhumell

The white away kit with distinctive green sleeve detailing and Parmalat sponsorship. This design represents Palmeiras during their successful late 90s period with its clean, elegant aesthetic.

Brazilian Classic
2002-2003

Parma Away Kit

Champion

The clean white away kit with navy and yellow trim from Parma's golden era. Worn by Adriano, Mutu, and Buffon during the club's last years of European prominence before financial troubles.

Golden Era
1999

Bayern Munich Away Kit

Adidas

The striking navy and red away kit with Opel sponsorship from Bayern's 1999 Champions League final appearance. Though they lost to Manchester United in dramatic fashion, this kit represents one of their strongest teams.

Champions League Finalist
1994-1997

Borussia Mönchengladbach Home Kit

Reebok

The classic black kit with white and green trim featuring the iconic Diebels beer sponsorship. This design represents Gladbach's mid-90s era as they maintained their Bundesliga status with style.

Bundesliga Classic
1998

England Home Kit

Umbro

The classic white kit with navy and red trim worn during the 1998 World Cup in France. Remembered for Michael Owen's wonder goal against Argentina and David Beckham's red card.

World Cup 98
1990s

Juventus Home Kit

Kappa

The iconic black and white stripes worn during Juventus' dominant 1990s era. This design was worn by legends like Roberto Baggio, Alessandro Del Piero, and Zinedine Zidane.
