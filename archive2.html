Classic Kits Collection

Relive the golden era of football with these iconic designs from the 90s and early 2000s

1999-2000

Lazio Home Kit

Puma

The sky blue kit worn during Lazio's historic 1999-2000 Scudetto-winning season. This kit saw the likes of Nesta, Nedvěd, and Verón lead the team to their second Serie A title.

Serie A Champions
1994-1995

Bayer Leverkusen Home Kit

Umbro

The classic red and black kit from Leverkusen's "Neverkusen" era. This design represents the club before their rise to consistent Bundesliga challengers in the late 90s.

Retro Classic
1996-1998

Liverpool Home Kit

Reebok

The iconic white pinstripe design worn during the 1996-97 and 1997-98 seasons. This kit saw Liverpool reach the UEFA Cup Winners' Cup semifinal in 1997.

Carlsberg sponsor
1995-1996

Manchester United Away Kit

Umbro

The infamous grey away kit that was abandoned at half-time during a match against Southampton. Despite its short lifespan, it became one of the most iconic kits in Premier League history.

Controversial
1998-1999

Manchester United Home Kit

Umbro

The famous treble-winning kit from 1998-99 season when United won the Premier League, FA Cup, and Champions League in dramatic fashion.

Treble Winners
1997

Palmeiras Home Kit

Topper

The green kit worn during Palmeiras' successful 1997 campaign. This design represents Brazilian football style in the 90s with its bold colors and simple design.

Brazilian Classic
2002-2003

Parma Away Kit

Erreà

The striking yellow away kit from Parma's golden era. Worn by Adriano, Mutu, and Buffon during the club's last years of European prominence before financial troubles.

Retro Shirt
2006-2008

Portugal Home Kit

Nike

Worn during the Euro 2008 campaign by Cristiano Ronaldo. The burgundy design with gold trim became iconic for Portuguese football and represented the golden generation's latter years.

Euro 2008
1998

England Home Kit

Umbro

The classic white kit with navy and red trim worn during the 1998 World Cup in France. Remembered for Michael Owen's wonder goal against Argentina and David Beckham's red card.

World Cup 98
1996-1997

Manchester United Away Kit

Umbro

The navy blue away kit with silver trim worn during the 1996-97 season when United won the Premier League title. Known for its clean, sophisticated design.

Premier League
2000-2001

Barcelona Away Kit

Nike

The striking orange and blue away kit from the 2000-01 season. Worn during Rivaldo's incredible season that included his famous bicycle kick against Valencia.

Retro Classic
1988-1990

West Germany Home Kit

Adidas

The iconic design worn during their 1990 World Cup victory. The distinctive flag-inspired pattern across the chest has become one of football's most recognizable designs.

World Cup Winners
