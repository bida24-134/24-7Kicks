Classic Kits Collection

Relive the golden era of football with these iconic designs from the 90s and early 2000s

1999-2000

Lazio Home Kit

Puma

The sky blue kit worn during Lazio's historic 1999-2000 Scudetto-winning season. This kit saw the likes of Nesta, Nedvěd, and Verón lead the team to their second Serie A title.

Serie A Champions
1994-1995

Bayer Leverkusen Home Kit

Umbro

The classic red and black kit from Leverkusen's "Neverkusen" era. This design represents the club before their rise to consistent Bundesliga challengers in the late 90s.

Retro Classic
2004-2005

Real Madrid Home Kit

Adidas

The classic white kit worn during the Galácticos era with Ronaldinho, Zidane, and Beckham. This design represents Real Madrid's pursuit of the most talented players in world football.

Galácticos
1995-1996

Borussia Dortmund Away Kit

Nike

The striking away kit from Dortmund's Champions League-winning season of 1996-97. Worn by legends like Matthias Sammer and Jürgen Kohler during their European triumph.

Champions League
1996-1997

Manchester United Away Kit

Umbro

The navy blue away kit with silver trim worn during the 1996-97 season when United won the Premier League title. Known for its clean, sophisticated design.

Premier League
1997

Palmeiras Home Kit

Topper

The green kit worn during Palmeiras' successful 1997 campaign. This design represents Brazilian football style in the 90s with its bold colors and simple design.

Brazilian Classic
2002-2003

Parma Away Kit

Erreà

The striking yellow away kit from Parma's golden era. Worn by Adriano, Mutu, and Buffon during the club's last years of European prominence before financial troubles.

1999

Bayern Munich Home Kit

Adidas

The iconic red kit from Bayern's 1999 Champions League final appearance. Though they lost to Manchester United in dramatic fashion, this kit represents one of their strongest teams.

Champions League
1994-1997

Borussia Mönchengladbach Home Kit

Puma

The classic white and green kit with the iconic Diebels beer sponsorship. This design represents Gladbach's mid-90s era as they maintained their Bundesliga status with style.

1998

England Home Kit

Umbro

The classic white kit with navy and red trim worn during the 1998 World Cup in France. Remembered for Michael Owen's wonder goal against Argentina and David Beckham's red card.

The iconic black and white stripes worn during Juventus' dominant 1990s era. This design was worn by legends like Roberto Baggio, Alessandro Del Piero, and Zinedine Zidane.
